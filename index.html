Why is Japan such a popular destination?

For most of my life, I've sought to travel to Japan, and explore some of the popular cities, namely Tokyo. But why exactly? What makes Japan so much more unique and fun?

Fresh Food

While sushi is often a form of cuisine enjoyed by many in the country, it's numerous types of food available that makes traveling to the country more unique...

  • Sashimi (Personal Favorite)
  • Rolls
  • Tempura
  • Ramen
  • Udon

Unique Entertainment

Contrary to common belief, Japanese entertainment has a lot of different forms of entertainment beyond simply anime and manga. styles and sub-categories that make it a cornucopia of film, television, and more...

  • Manga (Japanese Graphic Novels)
  • Anime (Japanese-produced Animation)
  • Tokusatsu (Live-action Film w/Special Effects)
  • Kabuki (Theatre-acting w/Masks)
  • J-Pop/City Pop (Japanese-produced Pop Music)

Scenic Landscapes

Away from the glow of the lights to illuminate the most populous cities, Japan's vast landscape and scenic landmarks help accentuate the nation's rich history and natural beauty.

  • Mt. Fuji
  • Itsukushima Shrine
  • Himeji Castle
  • The Hells of Beppu (Hot Springs Are Closed Off to Public)
  • Hiroshima Peace Park

What Do Travelers Have to Say?

While there is definitely much to do, and a lot to see in Japan, it helps to hear from those who have made similar journeys themselves. With a country so populous, and cities that cater to more dense populations, it helps to prepare ahead of time to not get lost. Here's what a few have said about their experiences...

  • "Always make sure to keep cash on hand. You'll need it for transport or payment for food."

    James Paxton - Toronto, Ontario (Canada)

  • "Never travel alone, especially during night hours. Many businessmen or thrill-seekers can be found intoxicated during the night, so it helps to walk around in groups."

    Carrie Hanson - Portland, Oregon (USA)
