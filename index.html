Why is Japan such a popular destination?

For most of my life, I've sought to travel to Japan, and explore some of the popular cities, namely Tokyo. But why exactly? What makes Tokyo so much more fun?

Fresh Food

While sushi is often a form of cuisine enjoyed by many in the country, it's numerous types of food available that makes traveling to the country more unique...

  • Sashimi (Personal Favorite)
  • Rolls
  • Tempura
  • Ramen
  • Udon

Unique Entertainment

Contrary to common belief, Japanese entertainment has a lot of different forms of entertainment beyond simply anime and manga. styles and sub-categories that make it a cornucopia of film, television, and more...

  • Manga (Japanese Graphic Novels)
  • Anime (Japanese-produced Animation)
  • Tokusatsu (Live-action Film w/Special Effects)
  • Kabuki (Theatre-acting w/Masks)
  • J-Pop/City Pop (Japanese-produced Pop Music)

Scenic Landscapes

Away from the glow of the lights to illuminate the most populous cities, Japan's vast landscape and scenic landmarks help accentuate the nation's rich history and natural beauty.

  • Mt. Fuji
  • Itsukushima Shrine
  • Himeji Castle
  • The Hells of Beppu (Hot Springs Are Closed Off to Public)
  • Hiroshima Peace Park
